# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want a friendly and safe community for everyone. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for all people, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Good behavior:**
- Be kind and respectful
- Welcome different viewpoints
- Accept constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Bad behavior:**
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private info without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct. Maintainers who do not enforce the Code of Conduct may face consequences as decided by other maintainers.

## Reporting

Report bad behavior to the project maintainers by opening an issue or contacting us directly. All reports will be reviewed and kept confidential.
